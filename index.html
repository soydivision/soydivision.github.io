    Introduction. #

    My name is Sadvakassov Bagzhan and I am … well. Human being. And this is my web sight. I have keen interest in set theory, formal logic, mathematical logic and related fields.
    However, this project is a recollection of usefull knowledge one can implement during a course of life, my thoughts on random topics, articles, contemplations and jokes. Topics include, but not limited to, human relationships, health, study, philosophy, politics, science. Read before you proceed. A prominent literature on any given topic is used as reference. One may find reading a blog rather productive than watching Youtube videos in a world of great distraction. Author (doomer) aims to provide a series of steps for his fellow brotheren to win this war against one’s own depressed/confused mind. This is collection of easy-to-follow how-to-change guides.
